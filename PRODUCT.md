# Zubda Product Context

## Product

Zubda / زبدة is an Arabic-first personal intelligence product. It creates one daily brief around each user's work, interests, region, watchlist, currency, and goals.

Tagline:
الزبدة مما يهمك

English support line:
The bottom line on what matters to you.

## Register

`brand` for the landing, pricing, public sample, marketing, and onboarding activation moments.

`product` for the authenticated app, daily brief reader, archive, settings, billing, admin, and generated intelligence UI.

## Users

The first user is a bilingual GCC-based ambitious professional, age 26 to 40, mainly in UAE or Saudi Arabia. They work in consulting, finance, tech, startups, government strategy, corporate strategy, investing, or leadership. They are mobile-first, heavy WhatsApp and LinkedIn users, comfortable with ChatGPT, and short on time.

They do not want more headlines. They want the bottom line before work, meetings, client conversations, investment decisions, and social conversations.

## Core Promise

Every morning, Zubda gives the user what changed, why it matters, what to watch, and what it means for their work, money, industry, or conversations.

The user should finish the brief and feel:
خلص، عرفت الزبدة.

## Product Principles

- One coherent brief, not an infinite feed.
- Personalization is the product, not a feature badge.
- Arabic should feel native, GCC-fluent, and easy to read.
- Mixed mode preserves natural English company names and business terms, but the UI should not randomly switch languages.
- Every major insight needs sources and a clear reason it was included.
- The product should make users smarter over time through plain-language explainers, tooltips, and glossary moments.
- The app should feel premium and sharp, but not stiff, formal, childish, or meme-like.

## Brand Voice

The tone is useful, direct, warm, and slightly witty.

Use:
- الزبدة
- وش السالفة؟
- عطني الزبدة
- بدون زحمة
- بدون حشو
- اللي يهمك
- وش تراقب؟
- وش أثرها عليك؟
- يعني إيش؟
- بالعربي البسيط

Avoid:
- Old-school formal Arabic.
- Corporate newsletter phrasing.
- English UI labels unless the user selected English.
- Developer-facing explanations in user-facing UI.
- Filler copy that repeats the heading.

## Anti-References

Zubda must not feel like:
- A generic AI summary app.
- A headline aggregator.
- A stiff finance dashboard.
- A government portal.
- A dark market terminal.
- A purple SaaS landing page.
- A meme app.
- A translated-English interface.

## Launch Scope

The MVP is a mobile-first web/PWA with:
- Firebase Auth.
- One primary intelligence profile per user.
- Multiple interest modules inside one profile.
- Daily private web brief.
- Email delivery trigger through Resend.
- Stripe Free, Pro Monthly, and Founder Lifetime plans.
- Archive, feedback, profile settings, billing settings, source transparency, and admin monitoring.

Out of scope for MVP:
- Native iOS or Android.
- WhatsApp and Telegram delivery.
- Audio briefs.
- Team dashboards.
- Multiple saved profiles.
- Premium licensed sources.
- Advanced chat over archive.
